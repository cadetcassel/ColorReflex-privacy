Privacy Policy

Color Reflex: Tap & Match  ·  Last updated: March 2025

Overview

Color Reflex: Tap & Match ("the App", "we", "our") is a casual color-matching game developed as a solo indie project. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. No account registration is required to play.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your photos, camera, or microphone
  • Your contacts or calendar
  • Any account credentials

Local Storage (High Scores)

The App saves your high score locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not shared with us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App

Advertising – Google AdMob

The App displays ads served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising platform operated by Google LLC. AdMob may collect and process certain device information to serve relevant ads. This may include:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID)
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • IP address (used for approximate location at country level)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. We do not receive or store any of this information. Please review Google's privacy policy for full details: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

Children's Privacy

Color Reflex: Tap & Match is suitable for all ages, including children under 13.

  • The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
  • No account registration, login, or profile creation is required.
  • No in-app chat, social features, or links to external social networks are present.
  • Ads served by Google AdMob comply with Google's Families Policy when the App is distributed in family-oriented categories.
If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at cadetcassel@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party SDK integrated in the App is Google AdMob. We do not use analytics SDKs, crash-reporting tools, or any other third-party libraries that collect user data.

Data Security

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to secure on our end. Your high score is stored only on your own device and protected by your device's built-in security (iOS sandbox, passcode / Face ID).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
